Frequently Asked Questions

How do I place an order?

Browse through our products, add items to your cart, and proceed to checkout. You can pay using various payment methods including credit card, PayPal, G-cash, or cash on delivery. Once your order is confirmed, you'll receive an email with your order details and tracking information.

What are the shipping options?

We offer standard shipping (3-5 business days). Shipping is free or can ange from 10-15 pesos. Track your order anytime using the tracking number provided in your confirmation email.

What is your return policy?

We accept returns within 30 days of delivery. Items must be unused and in original packaging. Simply contact the owner to initiate a return, and we'll provide you with a return shipping label. Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days after we receive the returned item.

How can I track my order?

After your order ships, you'll receive an email with a tracking number. You can use this number on our Track Order page or directly on the courier's website to see real-time updates on your delivery status.

Do you offer warranties on Abubots?

Unfprtunately, we do not offer warranties on our Abubots at this time. However , we ensure that all our products go through rigorous quality checks before shipping
